About Me

My name is Juan Acosta, I'm a Full Stack Developer from Montevideo, Uruguay.

I started programming at UTU in 2013, learning all the basics of programming and doing my first programs in Java, Visual Basic, C#. I graduated of "Bachillerato informatico" in 2016 with good qualifications. After that I get my first job as Mobile phone repair technician, and the Next year I started a Programer Analyst carrer in "Instituto BIOS" in 2017 and finishing at 2019. In BIOS I learned Web development with ASP.NET with C#, database management with SQLServer and documentation.

Now I'm learning for myself on internet at Udemy, with bootcamp frontend and backend courses, always trying to improve
